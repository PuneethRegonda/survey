Welcome to the 2026 Caltrain GoPass program.

The GoPass is an annual Caltrain pass that is offered to you by your organization. This is an exclusive benefit that allows you to travel through any zones at any time on Caltrain.
  
Before receiving your GoPass you are required to complete this survey, and to read and agree with the GoPass Use Acknowledgement terms and conditions. After you complete the survey, a confirmation email will be sent to your GoPass administrator who will issue you with your GoPass on Clipper.
 
Read the following instructions carefully:  

  • You need to complete this survey only once for the 2026 program at your current organization site.
  • It is vital that you complete the entire survey, as incomplete surveys won't be processed by the system, and your GoPass administrator will not receive the automated email notifying them that you are ready to receive your GoPass.
  • Please provide your organization or personal email address at the end of the survey and a copy of the email confirmation will be sent to you. Please forward this confirmation email to your GoPass administrator IF they require you to do so.
  • GoPass Users must tap on and off for every trip.


 
Enjoy the ride!
